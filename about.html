About Spirit Box

Spirit Box is a techy, minimalist chatbot experience designed to feel calm, efficient, and approachable. It blends clean typography, quiet motion, and a cyan accent to convey clarity and focus.

Design Philosophy

We prioritize readability and speed. The interface uses a single accent color (cyan) against a clean neutral palette, ensuring focus on your conversations with Spirit Box.
